Memoire

Capture emotions, preserve memories, and relive your story - Memoire is a digital time capsule crafted to hold life's most meaningful moments.

I designed Memoire to let users create private or public capsules filled with messages, media, and memories, locked in time and opened by choice. It's a warm, nostalgic space built for reflection, connection, and storytelling-wrapped in a vintage aesthetic that feels both personal and timeless.

Retro Shelf

Retro Shelf is a 90s-inspired mobile app designed to bring nostalgic vibes to modern streaming and content organization.

It allows users to collect and sort their favorite movies, games, books, and more-wrapped in vintage aesthetics and smooth UI. This passion project blends storytelling, layout detail, and classic visuals into a clean, engaging platform for people who love retro design and timeless digital experiences.

BookBuddy

BookBuddy is a community-driven book lending platform that connects readers through sharing.

I designed BookBuddy to make borrowing and lending books simple, social, and trustworthy. With user profiles, lending history, and location-based search, the platform encourages meaningful connections between readers-building a culture of sharing, discovery, and offline engagement in a digital-first world.
